Our mission is to drive central government organisations to adopt modern, interoperable, better value technology by providing the guidance, procurement routes and digital services they can't or won't develop on their own.

The criteria

  1. Standardising the way government buys and uses technology across its property estate
  2. Standardising the purchase and use of the hardware and software tools used day-to-day by every government worker
  3. Standardising how common back-office systems are used across government organisations
  4. Reforming complex contracting methods to fit the modern market
  5. Untangling the legacy architecture supporting public services and finding modern alternatives.
